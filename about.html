OUR AIM

We want fellow bodybuilders to be aware of alternatives should they get bored or have plateaud in their current training program. There are plenty of great training programs out there like Stronglifts 5x5. How PPL separates itself from the rest is that it centers the program based on movement-based divisions. The very nature of the program itself also means that any person who adopts the PPL split will be encouraged to go to the gym at the very least three times a week.
